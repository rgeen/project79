WHAT IS PROJECT 79?

Project 79 has secured contracts within an existing supply chain to purchase physical gold bullion and sell directly to a Dubai refinery at LBMA market rates.

It is anticipated that Project 79 will produce a stable monthly ROI that will range between 4-7%. Monthly rewards from Project 79 can either be airdropped or compounded back into the initial sacrifice.

Those that have sacrificed will receive an airdrop monthly or have the option to compound the 1st full month after the initial sacrifice period ends. The airdropped rewards will be in the form of a stable coin and will be relevant to the amount of stable coin sacrificed.
